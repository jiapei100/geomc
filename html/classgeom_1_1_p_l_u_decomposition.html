Detailed Description

template<typename T, index_t M, index_t N>
class geom::PLUDecomposition< T, M, N >

Computes the PLU decompostion for a matrix A, such that PA = LU.

L and U are lower and upper triangular matrices, respectively. P has dimension (LU.rows() x LU.rows()), and LU has the dimension of A.
